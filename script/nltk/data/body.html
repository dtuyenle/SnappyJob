Job Description for Organizational Effectiveness Specialist

  • Create reports on organizational structure and present data to upper management in order to make informed decisions on organizational structure.
  • Deliver communication plans, train hires in new positions, and organize the inter-departmental transfer of employees.
  • Analyze the internal structure of the organization and create strategies to maximize employee work output and effectively utilize employee skills.
  • Approach organizational strategy and HR issue with the overall company strategy in mind.
